Introduction

A broad-spectrum protective fungicide with contact action, used to protect fruits, vegetables, grains, seeds, and field crops against various fungal diseases. Mancozeb acts as a multi-site inhibitor, toxic to fungal cell membranes. It contains zinc and manganese, essential trace elements for plant metabolism. Its resistance to rainout and low UV degradation ensures high persistence of action.

Hazards Identification

  • Inhalation: Dust may irritate nose, throat, lungs.
  • Eye Contact: May cause moderate irritation.
  • Skin Contact: Prolonged exposure may cause dermatitis.
  • Ingestion: Very low toxicity, but not recommended.

First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air.
  • Eyes: Flush with water, consult doctor if irritation persists.
  • Skin: Wash thoroughly, remove contaminated clothing.
  • Ingestion: Give 2 glasses of water, seek medical advice.

Fire Fighting Measures

  • Flash Point: 137.8°C
  • Hazards: Dust may form explosive mixtures.
  • Extinguishing Media: Dry chemical, CO₂, water spray, or foam.
  • Dangerous Fumes: H₂S, CS₂, oxides of sulfur, nitrogen, carbon.

Handling & Storage

  • Avoid inhalation and skin/eye contact.
  • Store in cool, dry, ventilated place.
  • Keep container sealed, prevent contact with water.

Personal Protection

  • Use local exhaust ventilation.
  • Wear respirator, protective eyewear, gloves, and coveralls.
  • Wash hands and clothes after handling.

Environmental Info

  • Toxic to fish and aquatic organisms.
  • Avoid contamination of water sources.
